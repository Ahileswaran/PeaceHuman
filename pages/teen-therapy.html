  • Stay Active: Engage in physical activities to boost your mood and energy.

Tips for Parents

  • Listen Without Judgment: Create a safe space for your teen to express themselves.
  • Be Supportive: Offer encouragement and understanding during tough times.
  • Encourage Healthy Habits: Promote a balanced lifestyle with proper sleep, nutrition, and exercise.
  • Respect Their Privacy: Give your teen space while staying involved in their well-being.
  • Seek Professional Help: Consider therapy if your teen is struggling with mental health issues.
