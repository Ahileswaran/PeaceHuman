Tips for Getting the Most Out of Therapy

  • Be Open and Honest: Share your thoughts and feelings openly with your therapist.
  • Set Goals: Work with your therapist to set clear, achievable goals for your sessions.
  • Practice Self-Reflection: Take time to reflect on your progress and insights between sessions.
  • Stay Consistent: Attend sessions regularly and commit to the process.
  • Be Patient: Change takes time, so be kind to yourself throughout the journey.
